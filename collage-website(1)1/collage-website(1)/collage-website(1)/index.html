Courses We Offer

. Those Courses age convert the vast population into skilled and employable human resources in ICT of Bangladesh.
FCI provides two technology. they are :

Telecommunication Technology (TCT)

Telecommunication Technology is a highly important and in-demand field in the current information technology and communication sector. This technology forms the foundation of mobile networks, internet services, data transmission, and communication systems. Therefore, having skills in Telecommunication Technology provides good opportunities in the job market.

Computer Science and Technology (CST)

CST (Computer Science and Technology) is one of the most popular and in-demand technology-based courses of the present era. Its main goal is to develop student's skills in the field of information technology. Through this course, students gain practical knowledge in various areas such as computer programming, web design and development, software creation, networking, and database management.
