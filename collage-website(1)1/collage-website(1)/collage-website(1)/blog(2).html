Certificate in Telecommunication Technology (TCT)
This is to certify that Md. Jahid Hasan has successfully completed the Certificate in Telecommunication Technology (TCT) program at Feni Government Computer Institute for the duration of 2021–2025. This certificate recognizes his dedication, hard work, and commitment to gaining expertise in the field of telecommunication.
Program Overview
The Telecommunication Technology (TCT) course is designed to provide comprehensive knowledge and practical skills required for modern communication systems. Telecommunications is the backbone of connectivity in the digital era, involving technologies such as mobile communication, fiber optics, satellite systems, and advanced networking.
This program ensures learners gain a balance of theoretical knowledge and practical application, enabling them to meet industry demands and excel in their careers. Students learn to design, operate, and maintain telecommunication systems with efficiency, accuracy, and innovation.
Course Content
During his training from 2021 to 2025, Md. Jahid Hasan covered extensive topics, including:
Fundamentals of Telecommunication — Principles of analog and digital communication, modulation techniques, and signal processing.
Wireless Communication Systems — GSM, LTE, and emerging 5G technologies.
Fiber Optic Communication — Network design, installation, and maintenance.
Satellite Communication — Applications and operational principles.
Networking Fundamentals — TCP/IP, LAN/WAN configuration, routing, switching, and network security.
Microwave Transmission — Signal propagation and equipment handling.
Data Communication — Protocols, error control, and secure data transfer.
Hands-on Laboratory Training — Practical exercises with telecom tools and devices. Practical Training and Projects
Md. Jahid Hasan gained valuable hands-on experience by working on projects and laboratory exercises that simulated real-world telecom scenarios. These projects included network setup, signal analysis, system troubleshooting, and performance optimization.
This practical training equipped him with the capability to apply theoretical knowledge to real-life situations and address complex challenges in communication networks.
Skills and Competencies
By completing the TCT program, Md. Jahid Hasan has developed:
Technical expertise in telecommunication system design and maintenance.
Problem-solving skills for network issues and signal optimization.
Practical experience with modern telecom equipment and software.
Knowledge of wireless, optical, and satellite communication systems.
Ability to implement secure and efficient network systems.
Career Opportunities
Graduates of the TCT program are prepared for roles such as:
Telecommunication Technician
Network Assistant
Field Engineer
Radio Technician
Technical Support Officer
Network Administrator
This qualification also provides a strong foundation for advanced studies in Electronics, Communication Engineering, and Network Technology.
Significance of the Program
In today’s interconnected world, telecommunications is vital for economic development, technological progress, and global connectivity. Bangladesh’s growing ICT sector makes programs like TCT extremely valuable, producing skilled professionals to meet the demands of modern communication systems.
Conclusion
This certificate proudly acknowledges that Md. Jahid Hasan has successfully completed the Telecommunication Technology program with competence and commitment. His achievement reflects his dedication to acquiring skills essential for contributing meaningfully to the telecommunications industry.
